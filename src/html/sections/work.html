How we work

  • 1

    Enquiry received and images of surfaces to be laser cleaned to be sent by customer.

  • 2

    If customer requires ALC service a quote on a day rate will be sent

  • 3

    ALC technician arrives at customer facility and carries out the work with all PPE and safety curtains provided by us.

  • 4

    Job complete with zero clean up required. Work to be signed off by the customer and completion certificate issued by ALC.
